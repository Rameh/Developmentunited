About

Development United is a members-based online platform that
increases the visibility of development assistance. The platform gives organizations the information they need to make informed decisions in the field,
and facilitates partnerships among aid groups
all over the world.

Benefits

For the first time, we are going to gain a comprehensive understanding of who is doing what and where in oversees development assistance.
The world develops better, together.
Join our community!

Increased visibility provides organizations access to a wider donor base.

Increased visibility facilitates mutually beneficial Partnerships in the field.

Increased visibility stimulates a mutual sharing of information, knowledge and data to increase the effectiveness of aid delivery.

Platform

1. Register

Register your organization name, email and website, and you are one step closer to becoming visible. We will automatically generate a user-profile for your organization with this basic information.

2. Become Visible

Increase your visibility by logging the activities of your organization. Add the past, current and future projects of your organization. Logging your projects is quick and easy using our standardized templates. We only ask you for the information that donors and partners truly want to see.

3. Analyze Data

Search through our site using our interactive maps and data tool bars. Development United provides you with information that is specifically relevant to your activities in the field, and summarizes data into an easy to read and time-efficient document. We have collected data from local governments, the Abdul Jameel Latif Poverty Action Lab, Innovations for Poverty Action, Copenhagen Consensus: Haïti Priorise and much more.

4. Form Partnerships

After you have gained a comprehensive understanding of who is doing what and where, and read reports from the field's leading experts, your organization has the opportunity to act on this knowledge. The Development United platform is built to actively stimulate mutually beneficial partnerships. Using our "collaborate" button, your organization can look for partnerships to increase the impact and scale of your interventions.
